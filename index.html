About Matterhorn

The matterhorn zermattt Location and Shape. Situated in the Pennine Alps on the border between Switzerland and Italy, the Matterhorn's unique pyramidal shape and nearly symmetrical four sides make it stand out from its neighboring peaks. At a height of 4,478 meters (14, 692 feet), it towers over the village of Zermatt to the northeast and the Italian town Breuil-Cervinia to the South.

About jungfraujoch

Jungfraujoch top of europe Jungfraujoch is the highest accessible point in Europe - up to a staggering 3,454m height - thanks to its centennial railway, which was inaugurated in 1912. One stop within the tunnel offers spectacular views onto the glacier world outside through windows in the Eiger North Face. On the summit, visitors are greeted by a high-Alpine wonder world made of ice, snow and rocks.

About lucerne

Lucerne and mount pilatus Pilatus is a tall mountain peak towering over Lucerne. It's popular with tourists and locals for its spectacular 360 panorama views of many lakes and mountain views in Central Switzerland. At the top, you'll find a big observation deck, cafes and restaurants, lounge chairs, gift shops, etc.

About Lauterbrunnen

Lauterbrunnen valley Lauterbrunnen is a charming village nestled in one of the most picturesque valleys in the Bernese Alps, Switzerland. Not only is it famed for its beauty, with 72 waterfalls pounding down the valley rock faces, but also for the wide range of attractions and activities you can do in the area.

About Rhine

Rhine falls The Rhine Falls (Rheinfall) is breathtaking stupendous beauty and the largest plain waterfall in Europe. The falls are located on the High Rhine at Neuhausen am Rheinfall Village and just below Schaffhausen in the north of Switzerland. The Rhine's waters flow at the rate of 23 meters over a breadth of 150 meters. The gorge is quite deep and is the stretch of the river which is known for its many castles and vineyards

Explore Switzerland: Restaurants, Foods & Hotels

Discover Switzerland's culinary gems. These restaurants combine exquisite dining with breathtaking views.

RESTAURANT KRONENHALLE

Restaurant Kronenhalle is a renowned Swiss cuisine restaurant located in Zurich, Switzerland. Founded in 1924, it has become an institution for classic and traditional Swiss dishes.

CAFE DU SOLEIL

Café du Soleil is a charming French restaurant located in Geneva, Switzerland. Established over 400 years ago. The restaurant offers a cozy atmosphere with both indoor and outdoor seating, making it a popular spot for locals and tourists alike.

OLD SWISS HOUSE

Old Swiss House is a historic restaurant located in Lucerne, Switzerland. Established in 1858, it's known for its charming half-timbered architecture and rich history.

RESTAURANT SCHUH

Restaurant Schuh is a charming eatery located in Interlaken, Switzerland. Established in 1818, it's renowned for its delicious chocolate, pastries, and pastries, which are considered among the best in Switzerland and Europe.

Switzerland offers a delectable array of dishes. Here are some iconic foods you must try:

RACLETTE

Raclette few lines Raclette is a traditional Swiss dish and type of cheese. The name "raclette" comes from the French word "racler," meaning "to scrape," as the cheese is melted and scraped off onto boiled potatoes, pickles, and other accompaniments.

POLENTA

Polenta is a traditional Italian dish made from coarsely ground cornmeal. It is cooked slowly in water or broth until it reaches a creamy consistency, similar to porridge.

MALAKOFF

Malakoff is a delicious Swiss dish made from Gruyère cheese sticks that are marinated in white wine, coated in a light batter, and then fried until golden and crispy. It's often served as an appetizer or snack.

SAUCISSION VAUDOIS

Saucisson Vaudois is a traditional Swiss sausage originating from the canton of Vaud. Made from pork, lard, spices, salt, and sugar, it is typically dried and smoked. This sausage can be enjoyed cold or hot.
